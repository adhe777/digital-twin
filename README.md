This application acts as a "Digital Twin" for students‚Äîa virtual reflection of your daily habits. By tracking key metrics like sleep, study hours, screen time, and mood, the system uses AI to:

## üöÄ Key Features

### 1. Daily Routine Logging
-   **Input Data**: Log your daily stats easily.
-   **Smart Validation**: Prevents invalid data (e.g., negative hours) and ensures accurate tracking.
-   **Metrics**:
    -   üò¥ **Sleep Hours**: Track rest for cognitive recovery.
    -   üìö **Study Hours**: Measure academic effort.
    -   üì± **Screen Time**: Monitor digital distractions.
    -   üòä **Mood**: 1 (Low) to 5 (High) scale for mental well-being.

### 2. AI-Powered Dashboard
-   **Productivity Score (0-100)**: A calculated metric showing how effective your day was.
-   **Burnout Risk**: Real-time analysis (High, Medium, Low) based on your routine balance.
-   **Gemini AI Insights**: Personalized tips generated by Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash model.
    -   *Example*: "Prioritize sleep to boost your score by 15%."

### 3. "Digital Twin" Simulation
-   **What-If Scenarios**: Use sliders to adjust your current routine without actually doing it.
-   **Immediate Feedback**: See how changing Sleep or Screen Time *would* affect your Productivity and Risk *before* you make the change effectively.

## üõ†Ô∏è How to Use

### Step 1: Register/Login
-   Create an account to securely store your personal data.
-   Login to access your private dashboard.

### Step 2: Log Your Day
-   Go to the **"Log Data"** page.
-   Enter yesterday's or today's stats.
-   Click **Save Routine**. You will see a success message if everything is correct.

### Step 3: View Insights
-   The **Dashboard** automatically updates.
-   Check your **Weekly Trends** graph.
-   Read the **AI Insights** card for today's advice.

### Step 4: Simulate Changes
-   Go to the **"Simulation"** page.
-   Drag the sliders (e.g., increase Sleep by 1 hour).
-   Click **Simulate**.
-   The AI will predict the outcome: *"Productivity Score +5, Burnout Risk: Low"*
